About me

Hi, I’m Himanshu Goyal. I am pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science program at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am specializing in Computing Systems. I recently graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar after completing Dual Degree(B.Tech+M.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering. I work closely with Prof. Sudipta Saha on practical and scalability aspects of having Byzantine consensus in low-power wireless systems. In addition to it, I am also working on security & privacy aspects for large scale decentralised systems. Here, I am mainly focussing on developing privacy-preserving solutions for efficient distributive computation over IoT data. I am also closely associated with Decentralized and Smart Systems Research Group (DSSRG) at IIT Bhubaneswar.

I’ve spent some wonderful summers in both industry and academia via internships. In Summer 2020, I interned with Embedded Systems and Robotics group team at TCS Research & Innovation with Chayan Sarkar on building customized language models for cognitive robotics applications. I also had a short stint working with Arpita Patra at Cryptography and Information Security lab, IISc Bangalore, in 2020. In the Summer of 2019, I worked on workload characterization in Multi-tier Cloud Infrastructure at CNeRG - Complex Networks Research Lab, IIT Kharagpur under the mentorship of Sandip Chakraborty.

When I’m not working, I am generally lurking on reddit and twitter to keep myself updated about the recent trends in my research work. I would highly recommend following known researchers in your field to get fast access to the latest research directions/developments. I like to collect resources for the benefit of fledgling students under the Crypto Resources and Miscellaneous sections. The resources may range from basic to advanced level. I also maintain a blog that sporadically gets updated.

During my undergraduate days, I was associated with E-Summit, where I both participated in and organized several events related to Entrepreneurship. I was also a member of The Fourth Wall(Dramatics Society of IIT Bhubaneswar), where I participated in several open and stage acts. I thoroughly enjoyed being here; I am and will cherish it throughout my life.
